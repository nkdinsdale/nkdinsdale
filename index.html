Nicola K Dinsdale

I am a dPhil (PhD) student in the Image Analysis Group at the Wellcome Centre for Integrative Neuroimaging at the University of Oxford, where I research deep learning based approaches for neuroimaging analysis, supervised by Prof. Mark Jenkinson and Dr. Ana Namburete. I am funded by the UKRI EPRSC/MRC as part of the ONBI DTC.

My current research focuses on trying to solve the data harmonisation problem in MRI, using iterative unlearning to create feature embeddings which are invariant to scanner.

Research

I'm interested in computer vision, deep learning, and medical imaging. I am especially interested in exploring methods to overcome the barriers to clinical translatability of deep learning methods.
